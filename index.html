Viruses

Computer viruses are malicious programs designed to replicate and spread across computers. They can cause various problems like system crashes, file corruption, and unauthorized access to personal data.

.

Malware

Malware is a broad term that refers to any harmful software, including viruses, worms, trojans, and spyware. Its goal is to damage or disable systems, steal sensitive data, or gain unauthorized access.

Spam

Spam refers to unwanted, often harmful emails or messages. These messages are usually sent in bulk and can carry malicious attachments or links designed to deceive recipients.

Antivirus

Antivirus software is designed to detect, prevent, and remove viruses and other malicious programs from computers. It plays a vital role in protecting systems from cyber threats.

Plagiarism

Plagiarism is the act of using someone else's work or ideas without proper acknowledgment, presenting them as your own. It undermines originality and ethics, making it a serious issue in academics and creative fields.
